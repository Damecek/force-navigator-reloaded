Force Navigator Reloaded

Shortcut

Open the command palette with Ctrl+Shift+L (Windows) or ⌘+Shift+P (Mac).
You can change this in Chrome Extension Shortcuts.
Press the shortcut again or Esc to close the palette.

For advanced configuration open Settings.
